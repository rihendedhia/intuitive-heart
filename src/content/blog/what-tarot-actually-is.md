Tarot isn't about fixed predictions. It is an invitation to notice the hidden energy, the story you are telling yourself, and the next step you can take with greater clarity.

When we sit together, I listen to the shape of your questions. The cards help me reflect back what is already moving beneath the surface.

If you're unsure whether a reading is right for you, start with curiosity rather than certainty. The clearest readings are the ones that help you feel more connected to your own wisdom.
